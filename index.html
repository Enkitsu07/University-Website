Our Mission

To equip students with strong technical knowledge, problem-solving skills, and ethical values in computing and engineering, preparing them to innovate and lead in a digital world.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in computer studies and engineering, recognized for innovation, research, and contributions to industry and society.

About P.I.T.I

The Philippine Institute of Technology and Innovation is a premier higher education institution dedicated to advancing knowledge in computer science and technology while fostering a culture of creativity and problem-solving. Established in 1986, PITI has grown into a leader of innovation, offering programs such as engineering and computer studies

Degree Programs Offered

  • BS Architecture
  • BS Civil Engineering
  • BS Computer Engineering
  • BS Mechanical Engineering
  • BS Chemical Engineering
  • BS Industrial Engineering
  • BS Electronics Engineering
  • BS Information Technology
  • BS Computer Science
  • BS Data Science

Each program is designed to equip students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge for successful careers in their respective fields.
